How TYST protects you
1
Sender identity is unknown to anyone
Messages are sent through an anonymous relay. Our server only sees the relay's IP, not yours. Even if the server is compromised, your identity is not there.
2
We cannot read your message
Encryption happens on your device before the message leaves. Your private key never leaves your device — we have no access to it.
3
Delivery time is randomised
A random delay of 2–47 seconds is added so timing patterns cannot be used to trace you.
4
Destroyed after reading
5 seconds after the recipient opens the message, it is permanently deleted. No backup, no log.
