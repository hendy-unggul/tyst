// how it works
01
Encrypted before it leaves you
Your message is encrypted on your device using ECDH P-256 key exchange before it touches any network. Your private key never leaves your device.
AES-GCM 256-bit
02
Routed through a blind relay
The server only sees the relay's IP address — not yours. All identifying headers are stripped before forwarding. A random 2–47 second delay breaks timing correlation.
header-stripped relay
03
Destroyed after reading
5 seconds after the recipient opens your message it is permanently purged from the server. No backup. No log. No copy anywhere.
ttl: 5s post-read
04
256-byte padding
Every message is padded to a multiple of 256 bytes before encryption. Even the length of your message doesn't leak.
size-obfuscated
